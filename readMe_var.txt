-----------------------------------------------------
lavoro2cat was computed considering the variable 
s02_lavoro (question 2.1 from the survey), i.e.:
1: Si, in modo continuativo
2: Si, ma in modo non continuativo oppure No

-----------------------------------------------------
diffeconomiche2cat was computed considering question
14.5, i.e.,:
2: Low: Molto facilmente + Abbastanza facilmente
1: High: Con qualche difficoltà + con molte difficoltà

-----------------------------------------------------
istruz2cat was computed considering the variable 
s14_tit_stud (question 14.4), i.e.:
1: Low: Nessun titolo + Licenza elementare + Licenza di scuola media
2: High: Diploma o qualifica di scuola media superiore + Laurea/Diploma universitario o titolo superiore

-----------------------------------------------------
socioecon3cat was computed considering the variables 
diffeconomiche2cat and istruz2cat, i.e.,
1: low socio economic status (istruz2cat = 1 and diffeconomiche2cat = 1)
2: medium socio economic status (istruz2cat = 1 and diffeconomiche2cat = 2 or 
istruz2cat = 2 and diffeconomiche2cat = 1)
3: high socio economic status (istruz2cat =2 and diffeconomiche2cat=2)
